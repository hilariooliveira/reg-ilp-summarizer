Tank-led Iraqi troops invaded Kuwait before dawn Thursday, seizing the ruler's palace and other government buildings, Kuwaiti officials reported., The Iraqi government of President Saddam Hussein said the Kuwaiti government had been overthrown.
Kuwait's U.S. ambassador said the Iraqis occupied all government buildings in the capital and were moving south toward the country's oil facilities.
The United States, he said, would insist on "immediate and unconditional withdrawal" of Iraqi troops.
The invasion came a day after talks between Kuwait and Iraq collapsed in Jiddah, Saudi Arabia.
In recent days, diplomats said Iraq had massed more than 100,000 troops at the border.
