Saudi Arabia, an oil-rich ally of Kuwait, has been subdued in its reaction to Iraq's invasion of Kuwait.
Diplomats reported that Iraq had massed 100,000 troops on Kuwait's border.
REACTION: The U.N. Security Council voted 14-0 to condemn the invasion.
Iraqi President Saddam Hussein said the 100,000 soldiers who invaded Kuwait began to pull back Sunday .
The invasion was denounced by the United States and other Western nations and by the Soviet Union.
The Iraqi statement was also reported by the Iraqi News Agency in Baghdad.
Saddam also accused Kuwait of stealing oil from a field that includes land both sides claim.
