LONDON -- Following his takeover of the unprofitable New York Daily News, British publisher Robert Maxwell plans to raise around #250 million ($448 million) through the sale of shares in his Mirror Group Newspapers Ltd. of Britain.
Mr Kevin Maxwell and Mr Ian Maxwell, two sons of the late Robert Maxwell, are named as the other two people most involved in these transactions.
Maxwell Communication said last month that its management would be taken over by Kevin Maxwell, Ian's brother.
An autopsy is planned for today, and the cause of death is officially still unknown Details of Maxwell's death are sketchy.
